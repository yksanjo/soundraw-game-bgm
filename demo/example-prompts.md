## Pro Tips for Demo

1. **Start simple** - Boss fight music is the most impressive demo
2. **Show the share_link** - Click it to play in browser (visual proof)
3. **Mention the cost** - "DeepSeek is 90% cheaper than GPT for this"
4. **Show the reasoning** - The `deepseek_reasoning` field explains choices
5. **Highlight speed** - Music generates in ~30 seconds
